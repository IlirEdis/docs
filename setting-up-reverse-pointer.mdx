#### Configure PTR Records on your EDIS Global VPS Hosting

Reverse DNS or PTR records for either IPv4 and IPv6 addresses can be configured in the KVM management portal.

Locate VPS in the [Services](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php?action=services) menu and click "**rDNS"**. Administrate PTR records for every IP address provided with the VPS service.

"**Set"** configures and "**Unset**" removes a record from DNS.

<div class="note-callout">Get more in-depth background information in our [PTR Record blog post](https://www.edisglobal.com/blog/ptr-record).</div>
