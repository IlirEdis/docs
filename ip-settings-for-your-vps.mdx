#### EDIS VPS IP seettings, Network Info

You can view and manage your VPS's IP address settings in the KVM management portal. This is where you will find the IPv4 and IPv6 addresses, gateway, subnet, and DNS servers.

Locate your VPS in the [Services](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php?action=services) menu and and then click **"Network info".**
