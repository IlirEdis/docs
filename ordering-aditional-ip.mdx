#### Adding up to 10 additional IPv4 addresses to your EDIS VPS If you are interested to order, please share with us *** the primary IP address of your VPS** *** the number of additional IP addresses you'd like to order** The easiest way to get in touch is to **drop us a message** using the messenger in the right bottom corner. We'll take care of the ordering process and send you an invoice.

<div class="note-callout">The price for an additional IPv4 address is EUR 5,- a month.</div>

Once the payment has gone through, we will assign the new IPv4 address to your service. You need to manually configure the new IPv4 address in your operating system. We send an email with instructions.
