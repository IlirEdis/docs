# AutoInstall / Install / Reinstall a VPS

To install your VPS with a fresh OS, follow these easy steps below:

<Steps>

Login to your [EDIS VPS panel](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php)
Locate your VPS in the [Services tab](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php?action=services)
Click button "AutoInstall OS" to install your VPS with a fresh password and an OS of your liking

</Steps>

1.  Login to your [EDIS VPS panel](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php)
2.  Locate your VPS in the [Services tab](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php?action=services)
3.  Click button "AutoInstall OS" to install your VPS with a fresh password and an OS of your liking
