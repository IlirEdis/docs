## AutoInstall / reinstall a VPS with a fresh OS

To reinstall your VPS with a fresh OS, follow these easy steps below:

 1. Login to your [EDIS VPS panel](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php)
 2. Locate your VPS in the [services tab](https://manage.edis.at/whmcs/clientarea.php?action=services)
 3. Reinstall your VPS "AutoInstall OS" with a fresh password and the OS of your liking
