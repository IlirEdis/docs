#### Mouting an ISO image on your EDIS VPS

**"Mount ISO"** to choose and mount an .iso file. Choose a prefab .iso provided by EDIS or mount a custom .iso which first needs to be uploaded to the platform via the **"Custom ISO"** function. Select the boot device in a noVNC and proceed.
